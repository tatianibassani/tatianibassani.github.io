E-commerce Platform

Transforming online shopping with strategic UX improvements

Role: UX Lead
Duration: 8 months
Team: 4 designers, 6 developers

The Challenge

A major fashion retailer was experiencing declining conversion rates and high cart abandonment. The existing platform had a 2.1% conversion rate, well below industry standards. Users struggled with product discovery, checkout complexity, and mobile experience issues.

Research Insights

Our comprehensive research revealed critical pain points:

  • 73% of users abandoned carts due to unexpected shipping costs
  • Product pages lacked essential information and social proof
  • Search functionality returned irrelevant results
  • Mobile checkout process was particularly problematic

We analyzed 10,000+ user sessions and conducted 25 user interviews to validate findings.

Strategic Redesign

Product Discovery

Enhanced search with intelligent filters, visual browsing, and personalized recommendations.

Product Pages

Redesigned with high-quality images, detailed specs, reviews, and size guides.

Checkout Flow

Streamlined 6-step process to 3 steps with transparent pricing and multiple payment options.

Key Improvements

Smart Search

AI-powered search with visual filters, trending items, and predictive suggestions.

Social Proof

Integrated customer reviews, ratings, photos, and recently viewed items.

Mobile Optimization

Touch-friendly interface with swipe gestures and one-handed operation support.
