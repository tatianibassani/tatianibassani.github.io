Healthcare Dashboard

Streamlining patient care with intuitive data visualization

Role: Senior UX Designer
Duration: 5 months
Team: 3 designers, 4 developers, 2 doctors

The Challenge

Healthcare professionals were struggling with an outdated patient management system that scattered critical information across multiple screens. Doctors needed quick access to patient history, vitals, and treatment plans to make informed decisions efficiently during busy hospital rounds.

User Research

Working closely with medical professionals, we conducted:

  • Shadowing sessions with 12 doctors during hospital rounds
  • Interviews with 20 nurses about daily workflow challenges
  • Analysis of existing system usage patterns
  • Time-motion studies to identify bottlenecks

Key insight: Medical staff needed information hierarchy that matched clinical priority, not administrative convenience.

Design Solutions

Information Architecture

Redesigned data hierarchy to prioritize critical patient information and recent changes.

Visual Design

Created clear visual indicators for urgent conditions and treatment status updates.

Workflow Integration

Designed interfaces that fit naturally into existing medical workflows and protocols.

Key Features

Patient Overview

Comprehensive patient summary with vitals, allergies, and current medications at a glance.

Alert System

Smart notifications for critical changes, drug interactions, and follow-up requirements.

Mobile Optimization

Tablet-friendly design for bedside consultations and round documentation.
