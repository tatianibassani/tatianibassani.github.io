Food Delivery App

Revolutionizing the food ordering experience

Role: Lead Product Designer
Duration: 7 months
Team: 4 designers, 6 developers, 2 PMs

The Challenge

In a crowded food delivery market, our client needed to differentiate their app by focusing on local restaurants and community connection. Users were frustrated with long delivery times, cold food, and lack of transparency about their orders.

Research & Insights

Our research focused on understanding the emotional aspects of food ordering:

  • User interviews with 30 frequent food delivery customers
  • Restaurant partner interviews about operational challenges
  • Competitive analysis of 5 major delivery platforms
  • Customer journey mapping from craving to satisfaction

Key finding: Users wanted transparency, speed, and connection to their local food community.

Design Innovation

Local Discovery

Personalized recommendations based on location, preferences, and community favorites.

Real-time Tracking

Live order tracking with estimated delivery times and driver location updates.

Community Features

User reviews, photo sharing, and local restaurant stories to build community.

Key Features

Quick Reorder

One-tap reordering of favorite meals with smart suggestion algorithms.

Group Ordering

Collaborative ordering feature for offices and families with split payment options.

Restaurant Stories

Behind-the-scenes content from local restaurants to build emotional connections.
