Mobile Banking App

Redesigning financial experiences for 450K+ users

Role: Lead UI/UX Designer
Duration: 6 months
Team: 3 designers, 4 developers

The Challenge

The existing mobile banking app had a 2.1-star rating and high abandonment rates. Users complained about confusing navigation, outdated design, and poor accessibility. Our goal was to create an intuitive, secure, and accessible banking experience that would increase user satisfaction and engagement.

Research & Discovery

We conducted extensive user research including:

  • 15 in-depth user interviews
  • Survey of 500+ existing users
  • Competitive analysis of 8 major banking apps
  • Accessibility audit of current app

Key findings revealed that users prioritized security visibility, quick transactions, and clear account overviews.

Design Process

Wireframing

Created low-fidelity wireframes focusing on information architecture and user flow optimization.

Prototyping

Built interactive prototypes to test key user journeys and validate design decisions.

Testing

Conducted 3 rounds of usability testing with 24 participants to refine the experience.

Key Features

Dashboard Redesign

Simplified account overview with clear balance visibility and quick action buttons.

Enhanced Security

Biometric authentication and clear security indicators to build user trust.

Quick Transfers

Streamlined money transfer flow reducing steps from 8 to 3.
