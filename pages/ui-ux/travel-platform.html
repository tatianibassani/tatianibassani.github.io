Travel Booking Platform

Creating seamless journey planning experiences

Role: Senior UX Designer
Duration: 4 months
Team: 2 designers, 5 developers

The Challenge

Design a comprehensive travel booking platform that simplifies the complex process of planning multi-destination trips. Users needed to book flights, hotels, and activities while managing itineraries and group bookings seamlessly.

User Research

Through extensive research, we identified three primary user personas:

  • Business Travelers: Need quick, efficient booking with expense tracking
  • Family Vacationers: Require group booking features and activity planning
  • Adventure Seekers: Want flexible itineraries and local experiences

Pain points included fragmented booking processes, lack of itinerary management, and poor mobile experience.

Design Solutions

Unified Search

Single search interface for flights, hotels, and activities with smart filtering options.

Visual Itineraries

Timeline-based itinerary builder with drag-and-drop functionality and visual trip maps.

Group Booking

Collaborative booking features allowing multiple users to contribute to trip planning.

Key Innovations

Smart Recommendations

AI-powered suggestions based on user preferences, budget, and travel history.

Real-time Collaboration

Live editing and commenting system for group trip planning with conflict resolution.

Mobile-first Design

Progressive web app optimized for on-the-go booking and itinerary management.

Design Examples

Search & Discovery

Intuitive search interface with smart filters and visual destination cards

Booking Experience

Streamlined booking process with clear pricing and instant confirmation

Trip Management

Personal itinerary view with real-time updates and travel information
