Drawing

Exploring form, light, and artistic expression through traditional mediums

Drawing has always been my foundation as an artist. Through pencil, charcoal, and ink, I explore the interplay of light and shadow, the essence of form, and the emotional depth that can be captured in simple lines. Each piece represents a moment of observation, a study in technique, or an expression of creative vision.
