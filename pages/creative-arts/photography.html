Photography

Capturing moments through composition, lighting, and visual storytelling

From intimate portraits to sweeping landscapes, each image tells a story of human experience, natural beauty, or urban poetry.

Equipment & Process

I work primarily with digital cameras, though I occasionally return to film for its unique aesthetic qualities. My approach emphasizes natural light and minimal post-processing, allowing the authentic character of each scene to shine through. Whether capturing fleeting street moments or carefully composed landscapes, I believe in the power of patience and observation.
