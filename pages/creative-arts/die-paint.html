Die Paint

Vibrant textile art using traditional die painting techniques

Die painting represents my exploration into the ancient art of textile decoration. Using traditional resist techniques and natural dyes, I create unique patterns and color compositions on fabric. Each piece is a meditation on color theory, cultural heritage, and the unpredictable beauty that emerges when artist and medium collaborate.
